Open Source projects by YouAPT.eu

Here we'll collect all Open Source project by YouAPT, a socially responsible development company, devoting most of their revenues to create digital platforms and other project that can impact society in drastically positive ways.

For now, there's only one project, Agnost, aiming to remove distractions caused by holy programming wars and vicous hype cycles, by providing patterns to architect your software well de-coupling business logic from storage and frameworks and programming languages, to allow you to re-use and share code re-usable across different technology stacks and programming paradigms.
